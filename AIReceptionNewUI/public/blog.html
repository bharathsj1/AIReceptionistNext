‚ú¶ Quality Framework

Best Practices

Keep control while letting automation work for you. These habits keep quality high and response times low ‚Äî field-tested by top customers across thousands of setups.

üèÜ
Pro Moves

Recommended Workflow

The order that actually works in practice

STEP 01

Start in Review Mode

Use manual review for email and DM replies first. Graduate to auto-send only after 2 weeks of trusting the output quality.

STEP 02

Keep Knowledge Current

Update FAQs, pricing, and policies regularly. Stale knowledge means wrong answers. A 5-minute weekly refresh is all it takes.

STEP 03

Use Smart Escalation

Route VIPs and compliance keywords to humans every time. Automation handles volume; people handle the sensitive stuff.

STEP 04

Schedule Weekly Checks

Spend 15 minutes in Calls and Email Insights. Fine-tune prompts, update your voice, and catch edge cases before they become patterns.

STEP 05

Test Before Going Live

Run end-to-end with a teammate first. Check tone, handoff timing, and that the right person gets the right task with full context.

‚ú¶ Weekly Quality Checklist

Click items to check them off ‚Äî run this every Monday morning for best results.

  • Review the top 10 conversations from the past week.
  • Update FAQs with new questions that came up in calls or emails.
  • Add new objections or edge cases to escalation rules.
  • Confirm the handoff process routes to the correct owner with full context.
  • Check SLA compliance ‚Äî are follow-ups happening within the target window?
  • Tag outcomes on closed tasks ‚Äî won, lost, or needs follow-up ‚Äî for accurate reporting.

üí° Pro move: Assign one team member to own the weekly checklist. Accountability plus a 15-minute timebox keeps it from being skipped when things get busy.
