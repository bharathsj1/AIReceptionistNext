Email Manager

  3. Define reply tone and escalation rules for sensitive threads.
  4. Use ‚ÄúSuggest reply‚Äù in the inbox to approve and send.

Best practices

Keep control while letting automation work for you. These habits keep quality high and response times low.

Recommended workflow

  1. Start in ‚Äúreview‚Äù mode for email/DM replies; graduate to auto-send later.
  2. Keep your knowledge base current (FAQs, pricing, policies) so answers stay accurate.
  3. Use escalation rules: VIPs ‚Üí human, compliance keywords ‚Üí manual review.
  4. Schedule weekly checks in Calls/Email to fine-tune prompts and voice.
  5. Test end-to-end with a teammate before going live to customers.

Quality checklist

Review the top 10 conversations weekly. Update FAQs, add new objections, and make sure the handoff process routes to the correct owner with all details captured.
