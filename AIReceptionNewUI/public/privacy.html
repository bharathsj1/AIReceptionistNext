SmartConnect4u Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-31

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartConnect4u collects, uses, and protects information when you use our products, including AI Receptionist, AI Social Media Manager, AI Email Manager, CRM & Lead Manager, and AI Chat Bots (collectively, the "Services").

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide and information generated through use of the Services, including:

  • Account data (name, email, company, role, billing details).
  • Configuration data (business profile, hours, service details, automation rules).
  • Communication content (emails, social messages, calls, transcripts) you choose to connect.
  • Usage data (feature usage, device info, log data, performance metrics).

2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Provide and operate the Services.
  • Generate AI outputs and automations you request.
  • Secure and monitor the platform.
  • Improve product performance and reliability.
  • Communicate with you about updates, billing, and support.

3. Legal Basis (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, we process data based on contract performance, legitimate interests (such as service improvement and security), your consent, or compliance with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Data

We share data with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, communications, payment processing) only to operate the Services. We do not sell your personal data. We may share data if required by law or to protect SmartConnect4u, our users, or the public.

5. Google data use

When you connect Google (such as Gmail, Calendar, or Contacts), we access and process that data only to deliver the features you enable:

  • Calendar events to schedule, reschedule, and remind participants about bookings you request.
  • Email metadata and content to triage inboxes, draft responses you approve, and send messages you choose.
  • Contacts to keep your directory in sync for routing, suggestions, and replies.

We follow the Google API Services User Data Policy (including Limited Use). We do not use Google data for ads or sell it. Human access is limited to resolving support issues you request or where required to comply with law or protect safety. You can disconnect Google access at any time.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can adjust browser settings to manage cookies, but some features may not function properly.

7. Data Retention

We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. We may retain certain information for legal, security, or operational purposes.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

9. International Transfers

If you access the Services from outside our primary operating region, your data may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We take steps to protect data in transit and at rest.

10. Delete or revoke

You can disconnect Google access inside the app or by visiting Google Account → Security → Third-party access and removing SmartConnect4u. To delete your account and associated data, contact support@smartconnect4u.com; we will confirm and remove application data (subject to legal retention requirements).

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

12. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Services after changes means you accept the updated policy.

14. Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@smartconnect4u.com.
